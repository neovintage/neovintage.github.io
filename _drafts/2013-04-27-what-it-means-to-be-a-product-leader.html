For the last few months, I've moved into more of a product leader role at CarWoo! and man, it's been quite a ride. I've learned so much in the short time span of seven months.


This is what I've learned:
1. There are going to be people that push their priorities at all costs.
2. You really need to have some semblance of a tech background if you're a product manager
3. Understand who is your target market and understand where you are the product adoption phase
4. You can't please everyone
5. Say NO!
